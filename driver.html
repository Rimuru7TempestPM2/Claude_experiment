Driver Terms & Conditions

1. About the Platform

SwiftHaul Logistics ("SwiftHaul", "the Platform") connects independent transport providers ("Drivers") with clients requiring cargo delivery across Kenya. SwiftHaul does not employ Drivers — you operate as an independent service provider using the Platform to access job opportunities.

2. Eligibility

To participate as a Driver, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Hold a valid Kenyan driving licence for your registered vehicle class
  • Maintain valid vehicle insurance and a current inspection certificate
  • Own or have lawful possession of the vehicle registered on the Platform
  • Have a functioning smartphone with WhatsApp installed

3. Verification

Acceptance onto the Platform is subject to document verification by our operations team. Approval Status is set to "Active" only after all required documents have been reviewed and accepted. SwiftHaul reserves the right to reject applications or revoke Active status at its discretion.

4. Job Matching

Jobs are offered on a first-come, first-served basis. Once you accept a job, you are committed to completing it. Repeated cancellations after acceptance may result in removal from the Active driver pool.

5. Pricing & Quotes

Trip prices are calculated and suggested by the Platform based on route distance, vehicle class, cargo type, and current fuel index. These prices are estimates. The quoted amount is what is presented to the client. Final settlement is directly between you and the client after delivery. SwiftHaul does not guarantee minimum earnings per trip.

6. Payment & Commission

Clients pay you directly after delivery (cash or M-Pesa). A platform commission applies to each completed job. Commission remittance to SwiftHaul is expected after each job. Failure to remit commission may result in suspension from the Platform. Commission rates are communicated separately during onboarding.

7. Conduct

Drivers are expected to:

  • Handle all cargo with appropriate care for the declared cargo type
  • Communicate promptly with clients and ops via WhatsApp
  • Not subcontract jobs to other drivers without prior approval
  • Maintain their vehicle in roadworthy condition at all times

8. Liability

Drivers are solely responsible for the safe and timely delivery of cargo once accepted. SwiftHaul accepts no liability for cargo loss, damage, or delay arising from the Driver's actions or vehicle condition. Drivers are encouraged to maintain cargo-in-transit insurance where possible.

9. Termination

SwiftHaul may remove a Driver from the Active pool for: non-payment of commission, repeated job abandonment, misconduct, document expiry, or any conduct deemed harmful to clients or the Platform's reputation.

10. Data

By registering, you consent to SwiftHaul storing your name, WhatsApp number, vehicle details, and submitted documents for the purpose of platform operations. See our Privacy Policy for full details. Your data is not sold to third parties.

11. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Kenya.
