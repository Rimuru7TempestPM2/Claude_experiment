Client Terms & Conditions

Last updated: 2025. Please read before confirming a booking.

1. About the Platform

SwiftHaul Logistics ("SwiftHaul", "we", "us") is an on-demand logistics marketplace that connects clients requiring cargo transport with independent, verified drivers. SwiftHaul facilitates the matching and provides an estimated price — we do not ourselves provide transport services.

2. Booking & Confirmation

Submitting a booking request through this platform does not guarantee a confirmed booking. All bookings require explicit confirmation via WhatsApp from our operations team before a driver is assigned. You will receive a tracking code upon submission to monitor your request's status.

3. Pricing

The price displayed at the time of booking is an estimate based on the information you provide, including route distance, vehicle type, and cargo category. Final price is as quoted at confirmation. SwiftHaul reserves the right to revise quotes where the actual cargo, distance, or conditions differ significantly from the information provided.

4. Payment

Payment for transport services is made directly to your assigned driver upon successful delivery of your goods. SwiftHaul does not collect payment on behalf of drivers in the current service model. Payment methods accepted by drivers include cash and M-Pesa to the driver's personal number.

5. Cargo Responsibility

You are responsible for ensuring your cargo is accurately described, legally permitted for transport, and properly prepared for loading. SwiftHaul and its drivers reserve the right to refuse cargo that is:

  • Illegal under Kenyan law
  • Hazardous materials not disclosed at booking
  • Substantially different from the cargo type declared

Claims arising from loss or damage to cargo are a matter between the client and the driver directly. SwiftHaul facilitates matching and does not assume liability for cargo loss, delay, or damage.

6. Cancellation

You may cancel a booking before a driver has been assigned (status: PENDING_CONFIRMATION or OPEN) by contacting our operations team via WhatsApp. Once a driver has accepted your booking (status: ACCEPTED), cancellation may be subject to a partial fee at the driver's discretion.

7. Use of Your Data

Information collected during booking (name, mobile number, addresses) is used solely to facilitate the matching and delivery process. See our Privacy Policy for full details. We do not sell your data to third parties.

8. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Kenya. Any disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of Kenyan courts.

9. Changes to These Terms

SwiftHaul reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Who We Are

SwiftHaul Logistics ("SwiftHaul") operates an on-demand logistics marketplace connecting clients with transport providers in Kenya. This policy describes how we handle personal data in accordance with Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019.

2. What We Collect

When you use our platform, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Clients: Full name, mobile phone number, pickup and delivery addresses.
  • Drivers: Full legal name, WhatsApp number, vehicle registration plate, vehicle documents submitted during verification (National ID, driving licence, insurance, inspection certificate).

3. Why We Collect It

Personal data is collected for the following purposes:

  • To process and confirm cargo transport bookings
  • To match clients with suitable verified drivers
  • To enable clients to track the status of their bookings
  • To verify driver identity and vehicle compliance before onboarding
  • To communicate booking confirmations and updates via WhatsApp

4. How We Store It

Client and driver data is stored in access-restricted Google Sheets, hosted on Google's infrastructure. Access is limited to SwiftHaul operations personnel. We do not operate public-facing databases or data APIs that expose personal data in bulk.

5. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.
  • We do not use automated profiling or decision-making systems on your personal data.
  • We do not store payment card information — all payments are made directly between clients and drivers.

6. Your Rights

Under the Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)

To exercise these rights, contact our operations team via WhatsApp using the number on this site.

7. Data Retention

Booking records are retained for a minimum of 12 months for operational and dispute resolution purposes. Driver verification documents are retained for the duration of the driver's active status and up to 24 months thereafter.

8. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, contact us via WhatsApp at the operations number listed on this platform.
